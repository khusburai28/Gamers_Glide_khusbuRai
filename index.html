About

Gamers Arena is a one place destination for gaming geeks where they can play relaxing and entertaining games.There are six games in total: Tic Tac Toe : A game of Xs and Os where the player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner, 2048 : A game where the player slides the tiles on the grid in order to make a tile with the number 2048, Space Shooter: A game where the player has to shoot spaceships before they reach the shooter, Memory Game : A game of memory where the player has to match all the cards before the time runs out, Maze Game : A game where the player has to cross the maze in 30 secs, Snake Game : A game where the player maneuvers the snake which grows in length and has to avoid hitting the boundary. It is an easy to access website where the user can simply come and play any of their favourite games anytime and for however long they wish to play.
